How to Subscribe

  1. Click the "Subscribe to Calendar" button for your city
  2. Your calendar app will open automatically
  3. Select "Subscribe" when prompted
  4. The calendar will automatically update daily

Note: These calendars work with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, and most other calendar applications.
